Web Management and Development

5 Years, Part Time

For the past five years, I have volunteered my time and skills to three organisations in which I have a personal interest. I have managed the website for Heronbank Cat Rescue, completely redesigning it and creating content. This has been on a voluntary basis for over five years, as the rescue is a non-profit with limited funds, and it is a cause close to my heart. I have also volunteered my time with them in other capacities.

Additionally, I manage and update the website for Skin Secrets, a small business in Bedfordshire. I took over this role around two years ago when the business lost its domain name due to negligence from the previous hosting and management company. The owner was very distressed, fearing she had lost both the website and the domain. Although the domain had already been purchased by the time I was brought in, I successfully recovered the website and have been working with the owner to keep it updated ever since.

For the past year, I have also managed the website for my local scuba diving club, handling updates and making necessary changes. This role is voluntary, as I am a committee member. I am currently in discussions with the club regarding significant updates to the site, which we hope will increase both web traffic and membership.
