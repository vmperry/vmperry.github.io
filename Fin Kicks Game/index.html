Fin Kicks Game

Rules

Use the arrow keys to move the scuba diver .

Gain points by collecting clown fish , nudibranchs , starfish and shells .

Gain double points by collecting rubbish (plastic bottle, nets and cans ).

Lose points by hitting ship wrecks , sharks , jelly fish and eels .



Win the game by reaching 25 points.



It is game over if you hit the ammunition or your score reaches -10 points.
